RFID Solutions

Transform your tracking and identification processes with advanced RFID technology that provides real-time visibility into assets, inventory, and people. From asset tracking and inventory management to access control and supply chain visibility, RFID technology delivers unprecedented accuracy and efficiency. RFID Asset Tracking & Management

Eliminate time-consuming manual asset audits and gain real-time visibility into the location and status of your critical assets. Our RFID asset tracking solution automates data capture as assets move through your facility, providing instant updates and reducing asset loss.

Key Features:

  • Passive and active RFID tag options
  • Fixed readers at key checkpoints and portals
  • Handheld mobile readers for audits
  • Real-time asset location tracking
  • Automated check-in/check-out workflows
  • Integration with asset management systems

Benefits:

  • Reduce asset audit time by 95%
  • Achieve 99.5% asset tracking accuracy
  • Eliminate manual data entry errors
  • Reduce asset loss and theft by 75%
  • Optimize asset utilization rates

RFID Inventory Management

Transform inventory accuracy from 65-75% to 99%+ with RFID-enabled cycle counting and real-time stock visibility. Our solution enables you to conduct inventory counts in minutes instead of hours, while maintaining continuous visibility into stock levels and locations.

  • Handheld readers for spot checks and receiving
  • Anti-theft RFID gates and alarms
  • Real-time inventory dashboards
  • ERP and WMS system integration

Measurable Results:

  • Achieve 99% inventory accuracy
  • Complete cycle counts in 5% of previous time
  • Reduce out-of-stock situations by 80%
  • Lower inventory carrying costs by 25%
  • Improve order fulfillment rates
