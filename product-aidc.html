AIDC Products

Barcode Scanners

Industry-leading barcode scanning technology for fast, accurate data capture in any environment.

Product Range:

  • Handheld laser and imager scanners
  • Fixed-mount presentation scanners
  • Long-range warehouse scanners
  • Rugged industrial scanners
  • Wireless and Bluetooth models
  • Omnidirectional retail scanners

Key Features:

  • Read damaged and poorly printed barcodes
  • Fast scan speeds up to 1,120 scans/second
  • IP65/67 rated for harsh environments
  • Drop resistance up to 2.4m (8ft)
  • Extended battery life and hot-swappable batteries
